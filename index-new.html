Our Story

At Pie + Vine, we are passionate about all things food! We have created a place that is open and friendly to all ages. It has always been our mission to offer a menu that can be enjoyed during a harried workday or to celebrate a special occasion.

Every day we begin by making dough for our pizza and focaccia bread from our 15-year-old starter. We make our pasta, sauces, and stocks fresh daily.
