Sustainability

We are always looking for ways to reduce our impact on our community and planet. Beyond the everyday composting and recycling efforts we have committed to 100% electric vehicles for our delivery program. Even more exciting is the solar array that we installed to offset nearly 25% of our annual electrical usage!

Be a part of reducing single use plastic by purchasing a ROGUE TO GO box that can be re-used and exchanged with other participating restaurants in Ashland.
